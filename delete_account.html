Delete Your Fleex Driver Account

You can permanently delete your Fleex Driver account and all associated data directly from the app.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted within 30 days.

How to Delete Your Account

Open the Fleex Driver app on your device
Go to Settings (gear icon)
Scroll down to Account Settings section
Tap Delete Account
Read the warning and confirm deletion
Your account will be scheduled for deletion

What Data is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Driver profile and documents
  • Ride history and earnings records
  • Location data and tracking history
  • Account credentials and settings
  • Payment information and balance

Data Retention Period

After you request account deletion:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated
  • You are signed out and cannot log back in
  • All personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Some financial records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal/tax compliance
