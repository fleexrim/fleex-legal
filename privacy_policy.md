## 2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services to you.

### 2.1 Personal Information

When you register as a driver with Fleex, we collect:

- **Identity Information:**
  - Full name
  - Email address
  - Phone number
  - National ID number
  - Driver's license number and expiration date
  - Date of birth
  - Profile photo

- **Vehicle Information:**
  - Vehicle type (car, motorcycle, toktok)
  - Vehicle make and model
  - License plate number
  - Vehicle color
  - Vehicle registration documents
  - Vehicle insurance information
  - Vehicle photos (exterior and interior)

- **Financial Information:**
  - Bank account information (for earnings deposits)
  - Ride earnings and transaction history
  - Commission payments
  - Balance and payout records

- **Document Verification:**
  - Scanned copies of driver's license
  - Scanned copies of vehicle registration
  - Scanned copies of insurance documents
  - Scanned copies of national ID
  - Professional photos for identity verification

### 2.2 Location Data (CRITICAL - ALWAYS ACTIVE)

**This app collects location data to enable ride tracking and driver navigation even when the app is closed or not in use.**

We continuously track your precise location (GPS coordinates) when:
- You are online and available for rides
- You have an active ride in progress
- You are navigating to a pickup location
- You are completing a delivery
- **The app is running in the background**
- **The app is closed but you remain online**
- **Your device screen is locked**

**Location data is used for:**
- **Real-time driver positioning:** Passengers see your live location during rides
- **Accurate fare calculation:** Metered rides calculate fare based on actual distance traveled
- **Route tracking:** GPS checkpoints are logged for ride verification
- **Fraud detection:** We detect GPS spoofing, teleportation, and mock locations
- **Service quality:** Ensuring drivers follow optimal routes
- **Proximity matching:** Assigning rides to nearby drivers

**Location data retention:**
- **Active ride data:** Stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore during the ride
- **GPS checkpoints:** Retained for 90 days after ride completion for billing, support, and fraud investigation
- **Historical location:** Aggregated data may be retained for analytics (anonymized after 90 days)

**Location data sharing:**
- **Passengers:** Can see your real-time location during active rides
- **Fleex Admin:** Has access to location data for support, verification, and fraud detection
- **Google Maps:** Location coordinates are sent to Google Maps API for navigation and geocoding services

### 2.3 Device Information

We automatically collect information about your device:
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version (Android version)
- Unique device identifiers (Android ID, FCM token)
- Mobile network information
- Device settings (language, timezone)
- Battery status (to optimize background tracking)
- Screen state (on/off) for power management

### 2.4 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App:
- **Ride History:**
  - Pickup and dropoff locations
  - Ride duration and distance
  - Fare amounts (base fare, distance charges, stopped time charges)
  - Ride status updates (requested, accepted, in progress, completed)
  - Timestamps for all ride events

- **Driver Activity:**
  - Online/offline status and duration
  - Acceptance rate (rides accepted vs. declined)
  - Cancellation rate and reasons
  - Average rating from passengers
  - Total rides completed
  - Total earnings

- **App Interactions:**
  - Features used
  - Screen views and navigation patterns
  - Error messages and crashes
  - Performance metrics (app load time, GPS accuracy)

### 2.5 Photos and Media

We collect photos when you:
- Register as a driver (profile photo, vehicle photos)
- Upload verification documents (license, registration, insurance)
- Report issues or accidents (optional incident photos)

**Storage:** All photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage and are encrypted in transit and at rest.

### 2.6 Communication Data

We may collect:
- Messages sent to Fleex support
- Phone call logs (when you call passengers using in-app calling)
- Push notification interactions
- In-app feedback and ratings

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

### 5.1 Encryption
- **In Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **At Rest:** Data stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Cloud Storage is encrypted
- **Sensitive Fields:** National ID, driver's license, and bank account numbers are encrypted with additional layers

### 5.2 Access Controls
- **Authentication:** Firebase Authentication with email/password
- **Authorization:** Role-based access control (drivers can only access their own data)
- **Admin Access:** Logged and monitored for audit purposes
- **Device Security:** Single-device login enforcement (logging in on a new device logs out the old one)

### 5.3 Integrity Protection
- **HMAC Verification:** Metering data (distance, time, fare) uses HMAC signatures to prevent tampering
- **GPS Validation:** Unrealistic speeds, teleportation, and mock locations are detected and rejected
- **Audit Logs:** All data modifications are logged with timestamps and user IDs

### 5.4 Security Monitoring
- **Firebase App Check:** Ensures requests come from legitimate app instances
- **Crashlytics:** Monitors app crashes and errors for quick resolution
- **Suspicious Activity Alerts:** Automated detection of GPS spoofing, duplicate logins, and unusual patterns

**However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.** While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 6.1 Right to Access
- **What it means:** You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- **How to exercise:** Contact privacy@fleex.mr with subject "Data Access Request"
- **Response time:** Within 30 days
- **Format:** JSON export or PDF report

### 6.2 Right to Rectification
- **What it means:** You can correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **How to exercise:** Update your profile in the App settings or contact support@fleex.mr
- **Examples:** Correcting your phone number, updating vehicle information, changing profile photo

### 6.3 Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **What it means:** You can request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **How to exercise:**
  - In-app: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  - Email: Send request to privacy@fleex.mr with subject "Account Deletion Request"
- **Processing time:** Within 7 days of request
- **Data retention exceptions:**
  - Financial records (retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance)
  - Fraud investigation records (retained for 3 years)
  - Legal dispute records (retained until resolution)
- **After deletion:** All GPS checkpoints, ride history, photos, and personal information are permanently deleted after the retention period

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability
- **What it means:** You can receive your data in a machine-readable format and transfer it to another service
- **How to exercise:** Contact privacy@fleex.mr with subject "Data Portability Request"
- **Format:** JSON file containing all your data
- **Included data:** Profile, ride history, earnings, ratings, GPS logs

### 6.5 Right to Restrict Processing
- **What it means:** You can request that we limit how we use your data
- **How to exercise:** Contact privacy@fleex.mr
- **Note:** This may limit your ability to use the App (e.g., restricting location tracking prevents accepting rides)

### 6.6 Right to Object
- **What it means:** You can object to processing of your data for certain purposes
- **How to exercise:**
  - **Marketing emails:** Click "Unsubscribe" in any promotional email
  - **Push notifications:** Disable in App Settings or device settings
  - **Analytics:** Opt out by contacting privacy@fleex.mr
- **Note:** You cannot object to processing required for core app functionality (GPS tracking, ride matching)

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
- **What it means:** You can withdraw previously given consent at any time
- **How to exercise:** Disable permissions in device settings or delete your account
- **Effect:** We will stop processing data based on that consent (e.g., disabling location permission stops ride offers)
