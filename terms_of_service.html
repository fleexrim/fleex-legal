6.3 GPS Tracking and Fraud Detection

By using the App, you consent to continuous GPS tracking and fraud detection measures.

GPS Tracking Requirements:

  • Background location must remain enabled at all times when you are online
  • The App tracks your GPS coordinates every 10-60 seconds even when the screen is locked
  • GPS data is logged and stored for 90 days for fraud investigation and billing verification
  • You may not disable or interfere with GPS tracking while online

Fraud Detection and Prohibited Activities:

The following activities are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension:

  1. GPS Spoofing:
    • Using fake GPS apps or location-spoofing tools
    • Teleporting (moving unrealistic distances in short time periods)
    • Manipulating GPS coordinates to inflate fares
  2. Mock Locations:
    • Enabling "Mock Locations" in Android developer settings
    • Using location-faking apps to simulate movement
  3. Route Manipulation:
    • Deliberately taking longer routes to inflate metered fares
    • Creating artificial stopped time by unnecessarily idling
    • Ignoring passenger-requested routes without valid reason

Fraud Detection System:

  • Speed checks: Movements faster than 150 km/h are flagged as suspicious
  • Accuracy checks: GPS accuracy better than 1 meter may indicate mock location
  • Coordinate validation: Exact (0,0) coordinates are rejected
  • Teleportation detection: Impossible distance jumps are logged and investigated
  • Pattern analysis: Repeated suspicious behavior triggers admin review

Consequences of GPS fraud:

  • First offense: Warning and mandatory training
  • Second offense: 30-day account suspension
  • Third offense: Permanent account termination
  • Severe cases: Legal action and potential criminal prosecution

7. Ride Acceptance and Completion

7.1 Ride Requests

When you receive a ride request:

  • You see: Pickup location, destination, distance, estimated fare, passenger name
  • You have 30 seconds to accept or decline
  • You are not required to accept every request
  • Declined rides are recorded for analytics but do not result in penalties

7.2 Ride Progression

You must follow the correct ride workflow:

  1. Accept Ride: Tap "Accept" when you receive a request
  2. Navigate to Pickup: Use in-app navigation to reach the passenger
  3. Arrive at Pickup: Tap "I've Arrived" when you reach the location
  4. Passenger Boards: Tap "Start Ride" when the passenger is in the vehicle
  5. In Progress: Navigate to the destination using the app
  6. Complete Ride: Tap "Complete Ride" when you reach the dropoff location
  7. Rate Passenger: Provide a rating (1-5 stars) and optional feedback

7.3 Ride Cancellations

When You May Cancel:

  1. Passenger Not Responding:
    • You waited 5+ minutes at pickup location
    • Passenger does not answer calls or messages
    • Effect: Ride is relaunched to other drivers
  2. Rider Requested Cancellation:
    • Passenger verbally or via message asks you to cancel
    • Effect: Ride is marked as cancelled
  3. Other Reason:
    • Unsafe passenger behavior (intoxication, aggression)
    • Vehicle breakdown or emergency
    • Passenger destination violates terms (illegal activity)
    • Effect: Ride is marked as cancelled (requires admin review)

Cancellation Penalties:

  • Before pickup: No penalty if valid reason
  • After passenger boards: May result in penalties or suspension
  • Repeated cancellations (>20% rate): Account review and possible suspension
  • Fraudulent cancellations: Immediate account suspension

8. Fare Calculation and Earnings

8.1 Fare Types

Fixed-Price Rides:

  • Fare is predetermined before the ride starts
  • Based on estimated distance and time
  • Not affected by route taken or traffic delays
  • Displayed to you when accepting the ride

Metered Rides (Open Rides):

  • Fare is calculated based on actual distance and stopped time
  • Uses GPS tracking to measure distance traveled and stopped time
  • Fare updates every second in real-time
  • Final fare may differ from estimate

8.2 Metered Fare Components

Metered fares are calculated based on multiple factors including:

  1. Distance traveled - Longer trips cost more
  2. Time spent - Including time stopped in traffic
  3. Demand conditions - Surge pricing may apply during peak hours
  4. Vehicle type - Different rates for cars, motorcycles, toktoktoks

Fare Details:

  • Exact rates vary by ride type, vehicle category, time of day, and location
  • Current fare breakdown is shown in the app BEFORE you accept each ride
  • Fare configuration is controlled by Fleex and may change
  • You will be notified of any significant rate changes

8.3 Commission Structure

Fleex retains a commission from each fare:

  • Standard Commission: Percentage varies based on ride type, vehicle category, and region
  • Earnings: You receive the remaining portion after commission is deducted
  • Commission details are shown in the app for each ride before you accept

Example (illustrative only, actual amounts vary):

  • Total Fare: 100%
  • Commission: Percentage deducted
  • Your Earnings: Remaining amount after commission

Commission rates are subject to change with 30 days' advance notice to drivers.

8.4 Payment and Payouts

Earnings Tracking:

  • View real-time earnings in the App dashboard
  • See daily, weekly, and monthly earnings summaries
  • Track completed rides, average fare, and total distance

Payout Schedule:

  • Earnings are paid on a regular schedule (frequency communicated to drivers)
  • Payouts are deposited to your registered bank account
  • Minimum payout threshold applies (specified in driver portal)
  • Payout timing details are provided in the app
