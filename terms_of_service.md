## 8. Fare Calculation and Earnings

### 8.1 Fare Types

Fleex supports two fare types:

#### Fixed-Price Rides:
- Fare is **predetermined** before the ride starts
- Based on estimated distance and time
- **Not affected** by route taken or traffic delays
- Displayed to you when accepting the ride

#### Metered Rides (Open Rides):
- Fare is calculated **based on actual distance and stopped time**
- Uses GPS tracking to measure:
  - **Distance traveled** (every meter counts)
  - **Stopped time** (when vehicle is stationary in traffic)
- Fare updates **every second** in real-time
- Final fare may differ from estimate

### 8.2 Metered Fare Components

Metered fares consist of:

1. **Base Fare:** Minimum charge for the ride (covers first X km)
2. **Distance Charge:** Per-kilometer rate for distance traveled beyond base
3. **Stopped Time Charge:** Per-minute rate for time stopped in traffic (after grace period)
4. **Surge Multiplier:** Applied during peak hours or high-demand periods (if enabled by admin)

**Example Fare Calculation:**
```
Base Fare: 80 MRU
Distance: 10 km (base covers 2 km, charge for 8 km @ 15 MRU/km)
Stopped Time: 8 minutes (grace period 3 min, charge for 5 min @ 10 MRU/min)
Surge: 1.5x (peak hour)

Subtotal: 80 + (8 × 15) + (5 × 10) = 80 + 120 + 50 = 250 MRU
With Surge: 250 × 1.5 = 375 MRU
Rounded: 375 MRU (fare is rounded to nearest 5 MRU)
```

**Fare configuration is controlled by Fleex admin and may change. Current rates are displayed in the App.**

### 8.3 Surge Pricing

Surge pricing may apply during:
- **Night hours:** (Example: 10 PM to 6 AM) with multiplier (e.g., 1.5x)
- **Peak hours:** Morning rush (7-9 AM) and evening rush (5-7 PM)
- **High-traffic zones:** Market area, Centerville, airport
- **High demand:** Special events, holidays, bad weather

**Surge multiplier is locked at ride start time and does not change during the ride.**

You are notified of the surge multiplier when you accept the ride.

### 8.4 Commission Structure

Fleex retains a **commission** from each fare:

- **Standard Commission:** Percentage varies based on ride type, vehicle category, and region
- **Earnings:** You receive the remaining portion after commission is deducted
- **Commission details** are shown in the app for each ride before you accept

**Example (commission may vary):**
- Fare: 375 MRU
- Commission: 75 MRU
- Your Earnings: 300 MRU

**Commission rates are subject to change with 30 days' advance notice to drivers.**

- **You do not handle cash** (unless cash payment is enabled in your market)
